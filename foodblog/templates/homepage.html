About Philippine Cuisine

Philippine cuisine is a rich blend of flavors influenced by Malay, Spanish, Chinese, and American cultures. Popular dishes include Adobo, Sinigang, Lechon, and Halo-Halo. The cuisine is known for its bold flavors, use of vinegar, and a balance of sweet, sour, and salty tastes.
